How to Knit a Beanie

Your first wearable project using circular needles.

What You'll Learn

  • Knitting in the round with circular needles
  • The Rib Stitch (Knit 2, Purl 2)
  • Decreasing stitches for the crown
  • Using Double Pointed Needles (DPNs)
  • Making a pom-pom

Materials Needed

Size US 10 (6mm) circular needles (16"), DPNs, and bulky yarn.
