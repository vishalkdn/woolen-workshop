Advanced Cable Knitting

Add complex texture and depth to your creations.

What You'll Learn

  • How to read a cable chart
  • Using a cable needle
  • C4F (Cable 4 Front) and C4B (Cable 4 Back)
  • Fixing mis-crossed cables
  • Blocking cable knits for definition

Materials Needed

Smooth worsted weight wool (high twist helps definition) and a cable needle.
